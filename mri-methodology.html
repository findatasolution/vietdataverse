Market Reaction Index (MRI)

Mot chi so dinh luong do luong muc do phan ung cua thi truong VN30 truoc cac su kien tin tuc toan cau

1. Tong Quan

Market Reaction Index (MRI) la mot chi so do luong tac dong cua tin tuc len thi truong tai chinh Viet Nam, dac biet la VN30. Khac voi cac chi so cam tinh truyen thong (sentiment indicators) tap trung vao giai dieu ngon ngu, MRI truc tiep phan anh ket qua thi truong thuc te thong qua bien dong gia sau khi tin tuc duoc phat hanh.

Diem khac biet cot loi:
MRI khong chi danh gia "tin nay tich cuc hay tieu cuc" ma do luong "tin nay thuc su anh huong den gia VN30 nhu the nao".

Pham vi ung dung

  • VNINDEX: Chi so chung khoan VN-Index / VN30
  • GOLD: Gia vang trong nuoc (SJC, DOJI)
  • REAL_ESTATE: Thi truong bat dong san
  • BANKING: He thong ngan hang
  • FX: Ty gia, lai suat, hang hoa

3. MRI-1M va MRI-3M

De danh gia xu huong dai han hon, chung toi tinh toan MRI binh quan truot (rolling average) theo 2 khung thoi gian:

Chi So Cong Thuc Y Nghia
MRI-1M Trung binh MRI trong 30 ngay gan nhat Xu huong tam ngan han - cam tinh thi truong hien tai
MRI-3M Trung binh MRI trong 90 ngay gan nhat Xu huong trung han - moi truong tin tuc tong the
MRI-1M = (1/N) x Σ MRIi

voi N = so bai bao trong 30 ngay gan nhat
i = 1 den N
MRI-3M = (1/N) x Σ MRIi

voi N = so bai bao trong 90 ngay gan nhat
i = 1 den N

Cach Doc Chi So

MRI > 0 (Bullish)

  • MRI-1M > 0: Tin tuc gan day tich cuc
  • MRI-3M > 0: Moi truong tin tuc 3 thang qua thuan loi
  • MRI-1M > MRI-3M: Tin tuc dang cai thien

MRI < 0 (Bearish)

  • MRI-1M < 0: Tin tuc gan day tieu cuc
  • MRI-3M < 0: Moi truong tin tuc 3 thang qua bat loi
  • MRI-1M < MRI-3M: Tin tuc dang xau di

4. Phuong Phap Luan

4.1 Event-Study Framework

MRI mo rong framework event-study truyen thong bang cach chuyen doi bien dong gia do tin tuc gay ra thanh mot chi so da chuan hoa, co the so sanh giua cac bai bao.

4.2 Quy Trinh Xu Ly

  1. Thu thap tin tuc: Crawl RSS feeds tu cac nguon uy tin (CNBC, BBC, MarketWatch)
  2. Loc tin tuc: Su dung AI de loc 5 tin tuc co tac dong lon nhat den thi truong Viet Nam
  3. Danh gia impact: AI phan tich va cham diem impact_score (-1.0 den +1.0)
  4. Tinh MRI: Chuyen doi thanh thang diem -100 den +100
  5. Tinh MRI-1M/3M: Tinh trung binh truot theo khung thoi gian

4.3 Tieu Chi Danh Gia Impact

  • Quy mo anh huong: Tin tuc anh huong den bao nhieu linh vuc?
  • Tinh truc tiep: Anh huong truc tiep hay gian tiep den VN30?
  • Tinh cap bach: Tac dong ngay hay dai han?
  • Nguon tin: Do tin cay cua nguon thong tin

5. Ung Dung Thuc Te

5.1 Cho Nha Dau Tu

  • Theo doi cam tinh thi truong theo thoi gian thuc
  • Nhan dien cac su kien tin tuc co tac dong lon
  • Ket hop voi phan tich ky thuat de ra quyet dinh

5.2 Cho Nghien Cuu

  • Phan tich moi quan he tin tuc - gia
  • Nghien cuu hieu ung lan truyen thong tin
  • Do luong hieu qua thong tin thi truong

5.3 Tin Hieu Giao Dich

Tin Hieu Dieu Kien Hanh Dong
